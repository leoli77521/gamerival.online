Combat Arena brings you face-to-face with intense fighting action in a beautifully crafted HTML5 environment. Master various fighting techniques, learn combo attacks, and outmaneuver your opponents in this skill-based combat game.

How to Play

Controls: Use keyboard controls for movement, attacks, and special moves. Arrow keys for movement, spacebar for attacks, and specific key combinations for special abilities.

Combat System: Master the art of timing your attacks, blocks, and dodges. Learn to read your opponent's movements and counter their strategies effectively.

Victory Conditions: Defeat your opponents by reducing their health to zero while managing your own health and energy resources strategically.
